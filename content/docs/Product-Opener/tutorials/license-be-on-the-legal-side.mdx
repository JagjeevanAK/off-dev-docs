## Understanding and being compliant with our data licences

<Callout type="warn">
**Legal Compliance Required**

Understanding and complying with our data licenses is mandatory for all users of Open Food Facts data.
</Callout>

### License Overview

<Cards>
<Card title="Open Database License" href="https://opendatacommons.org/licenses/odbl/1.0/" description="The Open Food Facts database is available under ODbL" />
<Card title="Database Contents License" href="https://opendatacommons.org/licenses/dbcl/1.0/" description="Individual contents of the database are available under DBCL" />
<Card title="Creative Commons BY-SA" href="https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by-sa/3.0/deed.en" description="Product images are available under CC BY-SA license" />
</Cards>

<Callout>
**Image Rights Notice**

Product images may contain graphical elements subject to copyright or other rights that may, in some cases, be reproduced (quotation rights or fair use).
</Callout>
