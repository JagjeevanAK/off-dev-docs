## How It Works

<Steps>
<Step title="Data Collection">
Contributors take photos of price tags and products as proof of pricing information.
</Step>
<Step title="Location Verification">
All locations are verified through OpenStreetMap to ensure accuracy and consistency.
</Step>
<Step title="Price Validation">
Each price entry includes timestamp and location data for tracking price changes over time.
</Step>
<Step title="Community Access">
All collected data is made available through the API and web interface for public use.
</Step>
</Steps>
