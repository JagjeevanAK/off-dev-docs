## GCP Firewall Configuration

<Callout type="warning">
There is a firewall between internet and our GCP servers.
</Callout>

<Callout type="info">
**Important:** To enable `HTTP` and `HTTPS` traffic, it is **necessary** to tick the `Allow HTTP traffic` and `Allow HTTPS traffic` boxes in the VM settings.
</Callout>
